Keynote Speakers

Drs. Byan Drouwer (12:00-13:00)

This amazing young woman has a degree in 'Social Media Analysis', where see focuses on sentiment analysis using bigrams. Her master's thesis on the influence of using images of penguins on social media was cited more than a thousand times.

MSc. Bune R'ruijn (13:30-14:30)

Whereas much research on social media has been done using computers, Master of Science Bune R'ruijn refuses to do so. He has an alternative way of thinking and will never even touch a computer. His fresh perspective will be very interesting at the symposium.

Prof. Gas Berding (14:30-15:30)

Focusing on the impact of food when using social media, this grumpy man is a much respected professor in online sales. In the past year, he gathered data from over 200.000 companies and made these available at his website.
